QoS-SC 2016

International Workshop on Quality of Service in Smart Cities

In Conjunction with the IEEE International Conference on Collaboration and Internet Computing (CIC 2016)
Oct 31, 2016. Pittsburgh, PA, USA

Update

  • The QOSSC Workshop is partially sponsored by the Research Coordination Network (RCN) on Big Data/Smart Cities, which is supported by the National Science Foundation (NSF). One author (from the USA) of each accepted full paper will be considered for financial support, in the form of a RCN Fellowship for Big Data/Smart Cities, to present the paper at the QoS-SC Workshop and participate in the CIC conference. Depending on the availability of funds, the RCN Fellowship may include the conference registration and travel support. Early career investigators will have priority in receiving the RCN Fellowship.
  • Selected quality papers from the workshop will be invited to extend and submit to the top quality journal special issue in the area

Overview

The Smart Cities vision aims at exploiting advances in information and communication technologies to improve the quality of urban services, and through it, enhance the quality of life. Quality in the context of Smart City services can include performance, availability, reliability, scalability, interoperability, reusability, security, privacy, pricing and user experience.

We are interested in papers that address Quality of Service (QoS) related issues in the context of Smart City systems, applications and services including, but not limited to the following application domains:

  • Intelligent Traffic Management
  • Efficient Energy Management
  • Smart Transportation
  • Smart Buildings
  • Internet of Things for Smart Cities
  • E-Government Services
  • Emergency Management
  • Social Computing & Networks
  • Environment and Urban Monitoring
  • E-Health Systems

Paper Submission

We invite original research papers that have not been previously published and are not currently under review for publication elsewhere. Submitted papers should be no longer than 8 pages in two-column IEEE proceeding format.

Submission site: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=qossc2016
